The Great Sock Disappearance Mystery

Every laundry day, a sock vanishes without a trace.
Where do they go? A secret sock society? A portal behind the dryer? No one knows.
But one thing's for sure — somewhere out there is a pile of unmatched socks living their best solo lives.

Possible Explanations

  1. They fall behind or under the washer or dryer.
  2. They get stuck inside bedsheets or pant legs.
  3. They’re accidentally thrown away.
  4. They escape into another dimension through a hidden sock portal.

What These Solo Socks Might Be Doing Now

  • Forming a rebellious sock band.
  • Starting a new life in Sock-topia.
  • Enjoying freedom from laundry day responsibilities.
  • Organizing a reunion with other lost garments.
